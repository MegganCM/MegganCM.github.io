About Me

Hello! My name is Meggan and I am an Ecuadorian-American Artist. My current career goals involve following illustration and graphic design. My grand goal is to one day be able to be part of a production for an animated series and to make my own graphic novel series with my character designs.

I like to use my art as a vehicle to tell stories that explore various aspects of our humanity, whether it be the beauty of it or the monstrosities that are underneath the facades we live through from day to day. I love to draw what I see and take direct inspirations from all aspects of life. Using digital art, painting, drawing, and animation, I attempt to reimagine my own stories through bright colors and bold characters.

Ecuador for the World

Ecuador for the World (Ecuador Para El Mundo) is a dance group created in 2020 by Monica Reyes, Ligia Marca, and Meggan Coronel Marca. The group focuses on traditional Ecuadorian dances from all regions of Ecuador. During Hispanic Heritage Month, the group performs around New London County with the goal of educating the public about the beautiful diversity of Ecuador.
